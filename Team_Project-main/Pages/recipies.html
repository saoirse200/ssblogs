The Best Cookie Recipie

Post Written by S. Covaci, April 28, 2021

Here is the best chocolate chip cookie recipie ever! Enjoy these chewy chocolate chip cookies with a cup of tea. If you have time, leave the dough overnight for the best flavour and texture.

Ingredients You'll Need:

1. 150g butter , softened

2. 150g soft brown sugar , golden caster sugar, or ideally half of each

3. 1 egg

4. 1 tsp vanilla extract

5. 180-200g plain flour

6. ½ tsp baking powder

7. 200g chocolate chips or chopped chocolate

How to make them:
Step 1:

Mix the butter and sugar together using an electric whisk or hand whisk until very light and fluffy.

Step 2:

Beat in the egg and vanilla to the butter and sugar mix.

Step 3:

Fold in the flour, baking powder, chocolate and ¼ tsp salt as quickly as you can. Don’t overwork the dough as this will toughen the cookies.

Step 4 (optional):

For the best flavour, leave the mixture overnight: either cover the bowl and chill, or roll the mixture into balls and chill.

Step 5:

Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4 and line two baking sheets with parchment. Divide the mixture into balls. Space out evenly on the baking sheets, leaving enough space between each to allow for spreading.

Step 6:

Bake the fresh cookies for 8-10 mins and the chilled ones for 10-12 mins, or until browned and a little crisp at the edges but still very soft in the middle – they will harden a little as they cool. Leave to cool on the tray for a few minutes before eating warm, or transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

The cookies will keep for three days in an airtight container.

Enjoy!
